USAL's Background story

USA Self Learn (USAL) was born out of a simple yet powerful vision: to make the dream of studying in the USA accessible to every student without the heavy price tag. For too long, aspiring students have been exploited by agencies that take advantage of their desperation, charging exorbitant fees for services that should be available for free. USAL was created to put an end to this injustice and provide the support that every student deserves — free, transparent, and reliable. The founder, having experienced the struggle firsthand, saw how many agencies were taking advantage of students, offering empty promises and charging high fees for basic guidance that could easily be found elsewhere. With a deep frustration for this exploitation, USAL was formed as a direct response to those who profit off the dreams and ambitions of young people. What started as a small Facebook and Telegram group quickly grew into a powerful community, providing thousands of students with the tools and knowledge they need to succeed — at no cost. Unlike profit-driven agencies, we believe in empowerment, not exploitation. We offer personalized guidance, resources, and support, all for free, with no hidden fees or false promises. In just 9 months, USAL has helped thousands of students take control of their futures and successfully navigate the complicated process of studying in the USA. We’ve expanded our platform to Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok to make sure that even more students have access to the information they need, without having to turn to agencies that only care about making money. USAL is here to show students that it’s possible to achieve their dreams without falling prey to scammers. We’re not just a platform; we’re a movement dedicated to empowering students to make informed decisions and take control of their own destinies. Join USAL today, and let us help you build your future without the middlemen, the hidden fees, or the false promises.

What we built together

The mission of USA Self Learn (USAL) is to empower Nepalese students with the knowledge and resources they need to successfully navigate the process of studying in the USA. Through a YouTube channel, Telegram group, Facebook community, and website, USAL provides guidance on applications, interviews, scholarships, and student life—helping students achieve their educational dreams independently and affordably.
